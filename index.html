Vì Sao bạn Nên Chọn La Vie?

NATURALLY FILTERED

Natural mineral water forms below the earth’s surface, creating a source with recognized properties favorable to health. As it flows underground, the water undergoes changes in its physical and chemicals composition, acquiring truly inimitable traits. La Vie exhibits unaltered purity. Minerals are absorbed naturally as water flows through geological formations to its source.

La Vie Mineral Water comes only from carefully selected underground sources that are protected to avoid pollution and contamination. What starts out as rain soaks into the ground and is filtered naturally by the earth with a distinct composition of minerals to create our crisp, refreshing taste.

BENEFITS OF MINERAL WATER

La Vie mineral waters contain a unique composition 6 minerals, making them ideal for everyday , proper irrigation , which supports optimal operation of the mind and body. All this so you can enjoy fully operational mind and body !

GOOD TO USE DAILY

Nguồn khoáng La Vie có hàm lượng khoáng thấp (dưới 350 mg/l), không gây ra triệu chứng vôi hóa hoặc sỏi thận. Với hàm lượng khoáng nhẹ và ổn định, cáckhoáng chất có trong nước khoáng thiên nhiên sẽ giúp cơ thể dung hòa nhu cầu nước uống và khoáng chất mỗi ngày của cơ thể.
